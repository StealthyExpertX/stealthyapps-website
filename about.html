About

Hello, I'm Karl.

I build useful apps, plugins, extensions, and tools for real work. I care about clarity, privacy, and software that feels good to use.

Ground rules

One builder. Clear standards.

  • Built one project at a time.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or forced sign-up just to try something.
  • Keep data on your device whenever the product can work that way.

Why I'm doing this

Too many useful tools come with too much junk.

I got tired of software that wrapped simple jobs in tracking, bloated permissions, upsells, and friction. I would rather build tools that feel clear, useful, and respectful the first time you open them.

Stealthy Apps is where I ship that work. FillPro is the first public release, but the goal is bigger than one extension. I want this to be a home for practical software people trust and keep around.

How I work

What I want Stealthy Apps to stand for.

Real free tier

Free should stand on its own. Paid upgrades are there for extra room, advanced features, or people who want to support the work, not to turn the free version into bait.

Fair pricing

I price products around the value they save in time and frustration, and I do my best to keep them competitive. If I charge, I want it to feel fair.

No ads. No junk installs.

I am not building Stealthy Apps around ads, bundled installs, or dark patterns. I would rather make straightforward software people trust than chase the usual monetization tricks.
