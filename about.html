About

Hi, I'm Karl.

I'm a solo developer. I build small browser tools that solve one problem well, ask for the minimum, and stay out of your way.

In short

One person. Small tools. No tracking.

  • One developer. No team. No corporate layers.
  • No analytics. No telemetry. No email gate.
  • Your data stays on your device unless a feature clearly needs otherwise.

Why I'm doing this

Too many simple tools come with too much baggage.

I kept running into browser tools that wanted full-page access, account sign-ups, and analytics just to do something basic. I wanted to build the opposite: focused tools that ask for the minimum, keep data local where they can, and do the job without all the extra overhead.

Stealthy Apps is where I build those. FillPro is the first. More are already in active development. If something misses the mark, tell me and give me a chance to fix it.
