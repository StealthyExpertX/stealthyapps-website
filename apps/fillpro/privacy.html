Privacy Policy

FillPro Privacy Policy

Short version: what you save in FillPro stays in the extension on your device. I do not sell it, upload it, or quietly collect form values behind your back.

Effective: April 26, 2026 · Last updated: April 27, 2026

Short version

What actually matters.

  • Your profiles, rules, and uploads stay on your device.
  • FillPro does not collect form values in the background.
  • The only things that touch the outside world are billing and any support message you choose to send.

How data works here

What FillPro stores, skips, and sends.

What gets saved

  • Profile data: names, email addresses, phone numbers, and other fields you enter into profiles.
  • Captured form data: values saved when you choose Save current form.
  • Attached uploads: local file copies used for matching upload inputs you choose to fill.
  • Smart rules: custom match patterns and values for awkward field names.
  • Usage counters: free-tier profile count state stored locally.

What FillPro will not save or fill

Passwords, payment card numbers, CVCs, Social Security numbers, tax IDs, government identifiers, and one-time codes are off-limits by design. FillPro is not trying to be a password manager or a vault for sensitive data.

What leaves your browser

Your saved data does not leave your device. The only exceptions are:

  • Billing: if you upgrade to Pro, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle the payment. I never see your card details.
  • Support: if you use the support form, you can either send the message directly from the site or prepare it in your own mail app or webmail composer. Direct send delivers it to my inbox without opening your email app.

Deleting your data

Your saved profiles, rules, and uploads stay on your device until you remove them. I cannot inspect them or delete them remotely.

  • Delete profiles inside FillPro.
  • Uninstall the extension to remove extension-managed storage.
  • Delete exported files separately if you saved them outside the extension.

Optional diagnostics

If you use the feedback flow, FillPro can prepare an email draft with optional diagnostics you choose to include. That may contain page title, site path, visible field metadata, framework hints, browser details, and extension version. It does not include field values.

Permissions

FillPro asks for a small set of permissions because that is all it needs to work.

Permission Why FillPro uses it
activeTab Lets FillPro fill fields on the page you are using when you click Fill Page.
scripting Runs the fill logic on the current page.
contextMenus Adds the Save current form as profile right-click option.
storage Saves profiles, rules, uploads, and usage state locally.
alarms Handles the optional 3-day reminder. No outside network use.

Contact

Need a privacy answer?

Use the support page contact form and choose the Privacy topic. You can send directly to the right inbox or use your default mail app, Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, and copy options. Do not include passwords, payment card numbers, government IDs, or other highly sensitive information there.
