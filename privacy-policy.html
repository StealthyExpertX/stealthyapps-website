Privacy Policy

FillPro Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026

Overview

FillPro is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Collection

FillPro collects and processes the following data locally on your device only:

  • Profile data: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and other form fields you enter into profiles. This data is stored locally in Chrome storage and is not transmitted by FillPro.
  • Form data: When you use Save current form, field values are captured from the active page and stored locally.
  • Attached profile files: If you attach a resume, cover letter, or other file to a profile, FillPro stores an encoded copy locally so it can place that file into matching upload inputs when you choose to use it.
  • Smart rules: Match patterns and rule values that you create for awkward field names are stored locally with your profiles.
  • Usage counters: Profile counts for free-tier limits are stored locally.
  • Optional Pro license metadata: If you upgrade to Pro, FillPro stores the license status returned by ExtensionPay locally so it can unlock paid features. That metadata can include purchase or subscription dates and the account email returned by ExtensionPay for restore purposes.

Sensitive Fields

FillPro is intentionally designed to skip highly sensitive fields. It does not autofill or capture passwords, payment card numbers, security codes, Social Security numbers, tax IDs, one-time codes, and similar credential or payment inputs.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your saved profile data, captured form values, or attached files with third parties. FillPro keeps that data in your browser storage. Limited network requests only occur for optional Pro licensing with ExtensionPay/Stripe, or when you choose to open and send a support email draft yourself.

Optional Feedback Diagnostics

If you use FillPro's feedback tool, the extension can prepare an email draft to our support address. Diagnostics are only gathered locally when you explicitly opt in and click Send. Those diagnostics may include the current page title, site path, detected framework hints, visible field metadata, browser details, and extension version. Field values are not included, and FillPro does not collect or transmit diagnostics automatically.

Third-Party Services

  • ExtensionPay: If you upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by ExtensionPay/Stripe. FillPro may request your license status, subscription state, and the account email returned by ExtensionPay so Pro access can be recognized and restored. We do not see or store your full payment details.

Permissions

activeTabRequired to fill form fields on the current page when you click Fill Page.
scriptingRequired to run the form-filling script on the active page.
contextMenusRequired to add the Save current form as profile right-click option.
storageRequired to save your profiles and usage data locally.

Data Security

Your profile data is stored locally in Chrome extension storage on your device. FillPro does not transmit that data over the network. Access to locally stored data depends on the security of your device account and browser profile. FillPro also avoids filling or saving sensitive credential and payment fields by design, and we recommend not storing those values in profiles.

Data Retention

All profile data, smart rules, and attached upload copies are stored locally on your device until you delete them. Uninstalling the extension normally removes extension storage, but any profile exports you downloaded or original files you attached remain wherever you saved them.

Your Rights

You can view, edit, export, or delete all stored data at any time through the extension interface. No account or login is required for free use.

Changes

We may update this policy with new extension versions. Changes will be noted in the extension's changelog or public support materials.
